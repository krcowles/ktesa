Welcome to Tom and Ken's New Mexico Hiking Adventures!

The intention of this site is to provide the you, the viewer, a look at a variety of hikes, all undertaken by the authors in The Land of Enchantment. These hikes cover a broad, cross-state array of possibilities, all found in the diverse terrain of New Mexico - from short treks, to longer, uphill/downhill ascents. A number of the hikes occurred before the authors' acquisition of a GPS system, and hence will not have geomaps available to view. In those cases, a static map will be provided. Furthermore, some of the hikes occurred prior to certain environmental changes which have rendered them no longer accessible, or accessible only in modified form. Primarily:

  • Fires: Cerro Grande (Los Alamos 2000); Los Conchas (Valles Caldera, 2011); Gila (2012)
  • Floods: Bandelier (2011); Rinconada Canyon (2013)
  • Volcanoes & Earthquakes: we hope not!

One of the features of this site is that not all of the hikes listed can be found in popular hiking books, or even in on-line trail apps. Nonetheless, references are supplied when available. Below you will see a representation of the State of New Mexico, divided into various colored regions. Each region lists the number of hikes the authors have undertaken in that area, and clicking anywhere in the region will present a table listing those hikes. Alternately, for those wishing to view the entire index, they may click on the appropriate link below. The tables have sort capability, so that clicking on any header will sort the table in ascending order. Clicking again will re-sort in descending order, and further clicks will alternate the sorts. Note: All of the links on this site will open new windows (or tabs). The reference web pages ("Info Web Pages") provide sample pictures, trail descriptions, maps or tracks, and links to related materials. To see the Flickr albums containing the authors' photographs, click the album link(s).
