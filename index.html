Welcome to Tom and Ken's New Mexico Hiking Adventures!

The intention of this site is to provide the you, the viewer, a look at a variety of hikes, all undertaken by the authors in The Land of Enchantment. These hikes cover a broad, cross-state array of possibilities, all found in the diverse terrain of New Mexico - from short treks, to longer, uphill/downhill ascents. A number of the hikes occurred before the authors' acquisition of a GPS system. The GPS data comes from either a Garmin GPSMAP 64s handheld, or the EndoMondo mobile app. In addition, some of the hikes occurred prior to certain environmental changes which have rendered them no longer accessible, or accessible only in modified form. Primarily:

  • Fires: Cerro Grande (Los Alamos 2000); Los Conchas (Valles Caldera, 2011); Gila (2012)
  • Floods: Bandelier (2011); Rinconada Canyon (2013)
  • Volcanoes & Earthquakes: we hope not!

One of the features of this site is that not all the hikes listed can be found in popular hiking books, or necessarily in on-line trail apps. Nonetheless, references are supplied when available.
