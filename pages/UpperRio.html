The Upper Rio En Medio Trail, described in Day Hikes In The Santa Fe Area by the Northern New Mexico Group of the Sierra Club, is downhill to its point of departure near Aspen Ranch. At that point, the creek must be crossed and a slight uphill trail is followed to the ranch. In this particular hike, the authors missed the crossing on the way out and ended up traversing a steep ravine up to an irrigation channel, before discovering the correct route. One of the Santa Fe meetup group websites contains some further information on this hike. The trail follows alongside the creek on the downhill portion, along which are many lovely cascades, making for a very enjoyable hike.
