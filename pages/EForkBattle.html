The East Fork trailhead starts on Highway 4, as does the hike to Las Conchas, but cross the road and head south to start this one. Trail 137 has markers (some old and almost illegible), but some key junctures are not marked: about 1.5 miles in (near photo of Twisted Rock on geomap) a well-worn branch ascends while a slightly lesser-used branch continues down the gulley. Continue down the gulley (left branch)! Just past Jemez Falls picnic area, another branch occurs, but the left one ends quickly at a beautiful canyon overlook. This is worth the diversion. Then take the right branch down into the canyon. The USDA Forest Service maintains a link with up-to-date trail conditions worth visiting: E Fork Trailhead #137. Other sites, such as AllTrails.com also contain useful information.
