The Bosque del Apache offers even more than the fabulous opportunity to observe bird migrations, close up - it features numerous hikes in the area as well. These are generally not described in the hiking literature. The trails vary from river and marsh walks into the interior, to this Canyon Trail, and hikes into the nearby Chupadera Mountains. The Canyon Trail is the most unique and unlike the other trails which tend to feature the Bosque. Very interesting geological features can be observed close up, and the views from the top of the canyon are quite lovely. The trail forms an elongated loop.
