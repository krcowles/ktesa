The Bosque del Apache offers even more than the fabulous opportunity to observe bird migrations, close up - it features numerous hikes in the area as well. These are generally not described in the hiking literature, but information can be obtained from the Friends of the Bosque site, by clicking on their hiking link. The trails vary from river and marsh walks into the interior, to this Canyon Trail, and hikes into the nearby Chupadera Mountains.
