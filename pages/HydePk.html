Hyde Park Circle is a loop trail which starts in Hyde Park State Park, about 7 miles from Santa Fe. The trail is somewhat steep but provides nice vistas from the high point, after which it descends quite rapidly back down to Hyde Park Road. After crossing the road, a side trip can be made to the falls which is well worth the effort! Beautiful in summer months, it may be too steep for winter snow.
