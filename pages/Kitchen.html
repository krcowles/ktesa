NOTE: The author did not complete this hike, due to what was perceived as precarious trail conditions. Nonetheless, the hike into the box canyon was rewarding in terms of solitude and views. Early on, the hike leads through an area where dinosaur bones were discovered. The hike provides great views from the beginning, and is mostly easy up to the point at which it ascends the mesa top.
