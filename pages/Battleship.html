Battleship Rock is a picnic area north of Jemez Springs on Highway 4, which provides access to Forest Trail 137, East Fork Jemez River. The trail proceeds upward about 500 ft and 2 miles to Macauley Warm Springs, the destination for most. It continues past the warm springs to Highway 4 north, near Jemez Falls. There are actually three warm springs: the first is shallow and less ideal for immersing, though great for young children; the second is the most popular and inviting and includes a bit of a short falls; the third is not far downstream and less appropriate for soaking. While there are some online sites with YouTube videos, there are not many great references the author detected. Two other sources which contains some information are the books: Hiking New Mexico by Laurence Parent, and Guide to the Jemez Mountain Trail by Judith Ann Isaacs.
