Traders Trail is one of several trails that interconnect across the mesa near Taos Junction Bridge. It is very exposed, so if hiking in hot weather, leave early! The trail is a gradual slope down to an overlook of the rift valley, where the Rio Pueblo de Taos joins the Rio Grande. Looking south, one can see the bridge at NM570/NM567, which crosses the Rio Grande. The overlook area interconnects with the Rift Valley Trail, and it overlooks the Rio Pueblo de Taos directly, not the Rio Grande. Most online references available are for "Rift Valley Overlook".
