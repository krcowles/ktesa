The Las Conchas Trail is perhaps one of the most pleasant "short & easy" hikes around, with very lovely views along Jemez Creek. Since it is an "out and back" hike, one can make it whatever total distance suits, up to its 1.9 mile length (3.8 roundtrip), without losing any of its beauty. The trail does cross over the creek several times, always with a well-built wooden foot-bridge. As it is close to the Valles Caldera on Highway 4, the trip can be extended to that park for a full day of viewing. If you want to treat a visitor with a short and easy hike that is more than memorable, this is the hike!
