The Las Conchas Trail is perhaps one of the most pleasant "short & easy" hikes around, with very lovely views along Jemez Creek. Since it is an "out and back" hike, one can make it whatever total distance suits, up to its 1.9 mile length (3.8 roundtrip), without losing any of its beauty. The trail does cross over the creek several times, always with a well-built wooden bridge. As it is close to the Valles Caldera on Highway 4, the trip can be extended to that park for a full day of viewing. There is a nice site description on the Forest Service site.
