Pueblo Bonito is the largest excavated site in Chaco Canyon, and is considered central to the Chaco Cultural Site. It is the largest Great House with the most kivas in the canyon area. Walking to and around the site from the parking area consumes about a mile, and provides many interior and exterior views. Pueblo Bonito is also central to the astronomical discoveries made about Chaco Canyon. The National Park Service provides a Pueblo Bonito link for more information and photos.
