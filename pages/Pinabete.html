The authors did not find this hike in a trail guide or hiking book - rather it was word-of-mouth from a local Santa Fean. Online searches have revealed little of interest, perhaps because it is not a hike, per se, but rather a walk along the rim of Chino Mesa, near the location of the former Pinabete Tank. There are no trails. The views are nonetheless spectacular as you wind along the canyon edge. Travel to the tank area definitely requires a 4-wheel drive vehicle, and prepare for scratching and close encounters with rock-fields, junipers and cacti. The road gets progressively worse as you approach. The road is FR24, from NM62, off of Caja Del Rio Road, but watch for a fork, and take the beat-up trail to the right.
