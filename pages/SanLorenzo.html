San Lorenzo Canyon is a not-widely-known area of interesting geological formations. While there are a few trails, they are not named or otherwise classified, and exploration is up to the visitor. Trails are relatively short but provide some close-up views of unique rocks, as well as a few nice vistas along the higher portions of some of the trails. The Fish & Wildlife Service maintains a fact-oriented site, as well as Explore NM. Also, the book New Mexico Journey Guide by Kramer & Martinez has a section on the canyon. It is north of Socorro at the I-25 163 exit for San Acacia - see online or book references for details.
