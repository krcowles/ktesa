The Domingo Baca Trail, though not officially maintained, is nonetheless well-marked and hence easy to follow - though steep in parts, requiring an occasional bit of scrambling. Due to its elevation, the hike is demanding. The trail leads directly under the Sandia Tramway, from where one can spot bits of the wreckage shortly after. Once deep in the canyon, the view is limited. The wreckage is actually spread out over a wide area, but a plaque has been placed near the first piece encountered detailing the incident.
