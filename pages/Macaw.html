The Macaw Trail is the shortest and easiest walk in Boca Negra, and although it does not have many petroglyphs, the ones that are there are different and easily accessible to the viewer. The walk takes just a few minutes from the parking area, and has almost no elevation change. There are not many websites based on this short trail, but ProTrails does have a useful one.
