This hike is located at the northern end of the Sandia Mountains, near Placitas NM. While there is some elevation change, there are some level spots, and many great views looking west and north. Details of the hike can be found in the Day Hikes In The Santa Fe Area by the Northern New Mexico Group of the Sierra Club, as well as in the book Sandia Mountain Hiking Guide by Mike Coltrin, which provides useful GPS waypoints. The hike joins with the Piedra Lisa Trail at its summit. More information can be found online, particularly sandiahiking.com, which provides details on numerous hikes in the Sandias.
