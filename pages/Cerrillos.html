Cerrillos Hills State Park is just outside of the 'ghost town' of Cerrillos (actually, there are over a hundred residents). From I-25, the road in (Waldo Canyon) is part of the great viewing of the area. The Cerrillos Hills are full of old abandoned mines, now closed off, containing everything from galena to turquoise. The variety of turquoise from this area is a light blue and is given the name Cerrillos Turquoise. Hiking trails weave through the hills, so that various length trips are available.
