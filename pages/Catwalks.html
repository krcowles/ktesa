The Catwalk of Whitewater Canyon was constructed by the CCC to provide visitor access to the region (see links below). A short stretch of the hike takes you through the canyon on a suspended mesh "catwalk", and then exits and continues on a lovely forest trail. The canyon was flooded out and inaccessible in 2013, but is reopening in May 2016. The area is remote, and there are no "big" cities nearby, the closest being Silver City. The site is near Glenwood, NM.
