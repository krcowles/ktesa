The Strip Mine Trail is a relatively short trip through the colorful foothills of the northern Sandias. There is not much elevation change en route to the abandoned strip mine. From there, views north across the mesas and towards Cabezon peak are quite lovely. The trail is marked with a sign at the trailhead - a parking lot just off the dirt Forest Road 445 near Placitas. Information on the hike can be found in Sandia Mountain Hiking Guide, by Mike Coltrin, and online at sandiahiking.com. Pictures from the hike can be found in the Strip Mine Flickr Album.
