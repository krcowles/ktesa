Rio en Medio Lower - Proposed

Difficulty Round-trip Type Elev. Chg. Exposure Wow Factor Facilities Seasons By Car
Easy-Moderate 7.3 miles Out and Back 700 ft. Mixed Cascades None Not Summer

From EveryTrail: A very scenic short hike to a waterfall. Lots of plants and wildlife in the spring.

From AllTrails: Lower Rio en Medio Trail is a 7.8 mile out and back trail located near Santa Fe, NM that features a waterfall. The trail is good for all skill levels and primarily used for hiking, trail running, horses, and mountain biking. Dogs are also able to use this trail but must be kept on leash.
