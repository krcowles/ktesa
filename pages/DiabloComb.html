Parking for the Diablo Canyon hike is off of Old Buckman Road, a mile or two before reaching the Rio Grande. The steep lava cliffs are not only scenic, but also present rock climbers a great practice site. The hike proceeds through the canyon and down a wide arroyo to the Rio Grande. The hike is called the Rio del Caja Canyon in some older hiking references.
