The Ancho Rapids Trail is located 3.2 miles west of White Rock, NM on Highway 4. The first (approx.) 2 miles traverses the Pajarito Plateau (locally, "Powerline Mesa") before descending into Pajarito Canyon. The descent is about 1,000 ft where the trail proceeds to the banks of the Rio Grande at Ancho Rapids. The trail provides virtually no shade, so the best hiking times are spring and fall.
