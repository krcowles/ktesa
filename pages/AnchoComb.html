The Ancho Rapids Trail is located 3.2 miles west of White Rock, NM on Highway 4. The first (approx.) 2 miles traverses the Pajarito Plateau (locally, "Powerline Mesa") before descending into Pajarito Canyon. The descent itself is about 1,000 ft, near the end of which the trail terminates at the banks of the Rio Grande at Ancho Rapids. Although steep at times, it is not treacherous. The trail provides virtually no shade, so the best hiking times are spring and fall.
