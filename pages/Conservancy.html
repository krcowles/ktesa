The Nature Conservancy Trail on the outskirts of Santa Fe lies in the former water conservation district of the city. It is referred to as Santa Fe Canyon in the Santa Fe-Taos Hiking Guide by Bob D'Antonio. It is a very easy walk in the foothills with lovely views of the surrounding area. The Nature Conservancy site provides good historical context for the locale.
