The Frey Trail breaks from the Main Loop Trail and quickly ascends the canyon walls to the mesa top, and then on to Juniper Campground. The most pleasant part of the trip is ascent, though the views on top are also quite lovely. Looking back into the canyon provides some great views of the ruins, and across the canyon, the ascending and descending portions of the Frijolito Loop Trail can be seen quite clearly. The National Park website for the Frey Trail provides a bit more information, as do some of the trails sites, such as Explore NM and trails.com.
