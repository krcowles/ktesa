Buckman Mesa takes the hiker from the Rio Grande up to Otowi Peak and provides fantastic views of the White Canyon/Pajarito Canyon area en route. The Summit Post website provides some useful details, and there are other blog sites online of interest. The trail is described in the Day Hikes In The Santa Fe Area by the Northern New Mexico Group of the Sierra Club, but the hiking times given are inaccurate. From the arroyo especially, it is advisable to look for the stone cairns marking the trail up. The authors managed to take an incorrect route the first pass by over-analyzing the text from the book and going up a dead-end drainage. The hike is exposed, so spring, fall and winter are the best times to go.
