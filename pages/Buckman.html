TRAIL TIPS!

The trail, which branches up and away from the deep arroyo, is described in Book Reference (below) as "10 minutes" from a point in the arroyo. The authors were unable to interpret the time provided into a meaningful location and proceeded up a dead-end arroyo by mistake. A waypoint is provided in the geomap above (at red flag) indicating the correct location of the branch. It was marked with stone cairns.

Buckman Mesa takes the hiker from the Rio Grande up to Otowi Peak and provides fantastic views of the White Canyon/Pajarito Canyon area en route. The trip begins at the Rio Grande and follows a large and very deep arroyo to a point where it ascends the mesa between two of its "fingers". Once on top, the views of the valley are superb, and climbing further to the top of Otowi Peak rewards the hiker even more. There is a trail ascending Otowi, but if you wish to follow the edge of the mesa around to the point where the trail descends, you must find your own footing.
