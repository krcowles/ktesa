For this hike, the starting point is the same as the Upper Tesuque Hike: the Big Tesuque Campground pullout on Hyde Park Road, before the Ski Basin. The Middle Tesuque hike proceeds downward from the campground, but for a good portion of the trail (to the Borrego/Bear Wallows juncture), it does not run alongside the creek itself (refer to the map). As with the Upper Tesuque hike, there is not much online about this portion of the trail. Some of the sites blend in the hike with the Bear Wallow hike. See also "Borrego-Bear Wallow Hike" The most pleasant views of the creek are not far from the parking area at Big Tesuque Campgound.
