La Vista Verde is part of the Rio Grande Del Norte National Monument, south of Taos near Pilar. Part of this area is managed by the BLM. The hike here is beautiful and rather easy, gently descending a shallow portion of the Rio Grande River Valley and providing beautiful views and a few surprises (like the petroglyphs!). The BLM Orilla Verde site has significant area information, and it encompasses numerous trails in the region.
