The Fourth of July Trail begins at the campground of the same name in the Monzano Mountains, near Tajique, off of NM 55. The area is well known for its Bigtooth Maples, which provide beautiful reds and oranges in the autumn - a contrast to the mostly yellow of the aspens and cottonwoods so prevalent in the general area. The trail ascends and then veers west to join the Crest Trail, and when followed north takes the hiker to a nice view of the valley and nearby peaks.
